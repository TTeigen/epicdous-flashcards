What are Operators and how are these " + , - , *, / , % " used in JavaScript?

  • Operators are symbols that tell the compiler to perform specific mathematical or logical manipulations. In this tutorial , we will try to cover the most commonly used operators in programming.

  • + is used for addition in arithmetic or to concatenate strings.
  • - is used for subtraction in arithmetic.
  • * is used for multiplication in arithmetic.
  • / is used for division in arithmetic.
  • % is used for finding the remainder of after division in arithmetic.

What are Variables and Variables naming conventions?

What are Arguments and Paramaters?

An Argumentis an independent items, or variables, that contain data or codes.

A Paramateror a formal argument, is a special kind of variable, used in a subroutine to refer to one of the pieces of data provided as input to the subroutine.
